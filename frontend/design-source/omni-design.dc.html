OMNI PLAYLIST SYNC
CAT. OPS-001 · REV B · JULY 2026

Four libraries,
one groove.

A complete visual system for a self-hosted tool that touches people's music libraries and their credentials. The register is calm confidence — ink-black and putty hardware neutrals, one tangerine signal lamp, status always spelled out in words as well as color. Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music and Jellyfin appear only as small identity dots: guests in the house, never the wallpaper.

TRACK 01

Direction & the accent

four principles · three candidates · one recommendation
Calm over clever
One accent-colored action per view, ranked by commitment. Dry-run is quiet; execute is the loud one — and even it is just a solid button behind a confirm.
Status is spelled out
Every state carries glyph + label + color, never color alone. Feed kinds keep fixed glyphs (+ − ~ × !) so a colorblind user reads the same story.
Services are guests
Brand colors live only in 8–10px dots and soft badges. The app's own accent shares a hue with none of them, so this never reads as anyone's client.
Unhurried motion
120–320ms, ease-out, opacity + a 4px rise. The only loops are a soft pulse and a three-bar meter while a pass runs. Reduced motion collapses all of it to crossfades.
A · Tangerine
RECOMMENDED DEFAULT
The power-lamp orange of beloved music hardware, hot against ink. No service owns it — and since hold shifts to yellow, no status does either. Loud enough to be fun, warm enough to be trusted.
